Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the personal website of Lubomir Polascin ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website. This policy outlines our data handling practices and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable laws.

2. Data Collection and Use

We collect minimal data necessary to improve our website and provide services to you.

2.1 Automatically Collected Data (Analytics)

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors interact with our website. This service may collect:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website

This data is processed based on your explicit consent. You can manage your preferences via our Cookie Settings.

2.2 Voluntarily Provided Data

If you contact us via the contact form or email, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your message. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. We categorize them as follows:

  • Strictly Necessary: Essential for the website to function (e.g., saving your cookie preferences).
  • Analytics: Help us understand website usage (Google Analytics). These are optional.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners and trusted affiliates.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.
